Methodology: Course completion is measured against each learner's individual target (120 ac.h. as standard; Arthur Badin's target is 101 ac.h. because 18.62 h. were already completed in his C2 group). Each chart starts from the learner's own course start month through May 2026. Pace is the average academic hours per month over the last 3 months (Mar-May 2026); the cohort flags any active learner pacing below 4 h/month as 'at risk'. Forecast finish projects the month a learner will reach their target at current pace. Individual midterm assessments use a narrative format; group midterm uses six skill percentages plus Motivation and Self-Study scores. For on-hold learners no expected finish date is set. Cost methodology: Individual learners are billed at EUR 30 per academic hour. Group learners are billed at EUR 27 per academic hour; the per-learner group total is each member's own attendance × EUR 27 (so two members of the same group may differ if their attendance differs). "Total invested" sums individual and group spend without double-counting. "Cost per completed hour" equals total spend divided by total academic hours delivered. "Projected spend to completion" extrapolates current pace × remaining hours × applicable rate.
